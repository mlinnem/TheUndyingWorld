Direct to LLM (experimental)
Begin dialog with the GM immediately. To generate a random game world, first say something to the effect of "In the upcoming world generation sequence, please generate a game that occurs in [wherever]. Please confirm that you understand.". You might also be able to tell it other things (make this world easier than usual, etc.) although depending on the request this may or may not work. Then enter the command 'run_boot_sequence' exactly as written. If all goes well a new game world should appear in your listings in around 30 minutes.
